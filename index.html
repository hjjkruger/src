The Classic Experience

Observe, Don't Disturb.

Since 1999, we have operated with a simple philosophy: we are guests in their home. Our trips in Walker Bay offer a stable, family-friendly platform to witness the Southern Right Whales as they return to our shallow waters to mate and calve.

Our catamaran, Miroshca, is designed for comfort, offering 360-degree views and a "silent running" mode that allows the whales to approach us on their own terms.
