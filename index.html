Since 1999, Southern Right Charters has operated with a simple philosophy: we are guests in their home. Our trips in Walker Bay offer a stable, family-friendly platform to witness the Southern Right Whales.

Our catamaran, Miroshca, is designed for comfort, offering 360-degree views and a "silent running" mode.
